Who should participate in my care?
Cancer Care and Coping Services

Doctors who develop the treatment plan

  • Surgeons perform lumpectomies or mastectomies.
  • Radiation oncologists provide radiation therapy.
  • Medical oncologists manage chemotherapy and hormone therapy.

Other specialists to consult

  • Plastic surgeons help restore breast shape after surgery.
  • Genetic counselors coordinate genetic testing.
  • Reproductive specialists (fertility experts) discuss options for having children after cancer treatment.

Healthcare professionals who support your care

  • Advanced nurse practitioners (ANPs) organize medical management, including prescribing, managing side effects, ordering tests, and performing procedures.
  • Nurse navigators organize care and support, including scheduling appointments; removing barriers (financial, transportation); providing patient education; and identifying services for emotional support.
  • Research nurses oversee clinical trial integrity; screen patients; manage study protocols; collect data; and monitor for trial-specific side effects.
