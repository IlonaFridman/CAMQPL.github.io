Complementary and Integrative Medicine

What is Complementary and Integrative Treatment

Complementary and integrative treatments include a wide range of therapies such as acupuncture, meditation, herbal remedies, yoga, massage, and nutrition-based practices. To help you navigate complementary and integrative treatment, we assembled some therapies from evidence-based sources of information in the table below.

Exploring complementary and integrative therapies can be a valuable part of your healthcare journey. We encourage you to explore them and talk with your healthcare team—we’re here to support you and answer any questions.

Important: Always check with your doctor before starting any new therapy or supplement, as some may reduce the effectiveness of your treatment or cause harmful side effects.
